Welcome to PeerMentor Connect!

Find Your Mentor

An online platform that simulates the experience of choosing an advisor or tutor at a physical center. Select and book sessions with expert mentors to receive personalized guidance and support for your academic and career goals.

Search Mentor by Category

Explore mentors based on their role, years of experience, languages spoken, and specialized services. Our platform allows you to find the perfect mentor for your needs, with detailed profiles and availability displayed for a seamless mentoring experience. Choose the right expert to guide your academic and career journey with ease.

Book Your Mentor Online

Easily book your mentoring sessions online by selecting the service you need, and choosing the date and time that work best for you. Some sessions can be scheduled instantly, while others may require confirmation from the mentor. Booking your personalized mentoring support is straightforward and convenient.

About PeerMentor Connect


PeerMentor Connect (also known as student mentoring and academic support) is a platform designed to connect students with experienced mentors, allowing them to benefit from personalized guidance and expertise. PeerMentor Connect helps students navigate their educational journey by providing tailored advice, career insights, and skill development support. Unlike traditional tutoring, mentorship through PeerMentor Connect focuses on holistic growth, offering students a collaborative approach to achieving their academic and professional goals. The service is ideal for students seeking targeted help and valuable connections to enhance their learning experience and future opportunities.
